Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. This page explains how PVB.LLC (“PVB,” “we,” or “our”) collects, uses, and protects your information when you interact with our services.

Introduction

Welcome to PVB, a platform offering tools and software designed to enhance streaming performance and engagement on the Parti platform. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle your personal data and the choices you have regarding its use.

How We Use Your Information

  • Service Delivery: To operate, maintain, and improve our platform and products.

Sharing of Information

  • Third-Party Services: We may share limited data with secure and trusted partners (such as payment processors or analytics providers) to facilitate services on our behalf.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information to comply with applicable laws, regulations, or lawful requests from authorities.

Data Security

We apply modern security practices—including encryption and restricted data access—to safeguard your information. However, no system is entirely immune from risk, and we cannot guarantee absolute protection.

Your Rights

  • Access: You may request details about the personal data we hold on you.
  • Correction: You may request corrections to inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Deletion: You may request that we delete your data, subject to retention requirements under applicable law.

We may modify this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect service or legal updates. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised date. Continued use of our services after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

Responsible Use of PVB Services
